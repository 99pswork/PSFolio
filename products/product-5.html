Jewelshoot

AI photoshoots for jewellery — upload one photo, get catalog-ready model shots, e-commerce stills, and Instagram reels in under a minute. No studio, no photographer, no waiting.

Visit Jewelshoot Get In Touch

The Problem

Jewellery photography is absurdly expensive. A single product shoot day with a model, studio, photographer, and post-production can cost ₹30,000–₹1,00,000+. For small and mid-sized jewellers, this means most of their inventory never gets a professional photo.

The result? Beautiful pieces get listed with flat, lifeless images — or worse, phone photos against a bedsheet. E-commerce conversions suffer. Instagram engagement drops. Catalogs look amateur. And video reels? Forget it — those require an entirely separate production pipeline.

Jewellers needed a way to go from a single phone photo to catalog-ready model shots, white-background e-commerce stills, and Instagram reels — without booking a studio or waiting days for delivery.

How It Works

Step 1

Upload One Photo

Take a single photo of your jewellery piece — any angle, any lighting, any background. Our AI handles the rest. No need for a lightbox, white backdrop, or fancy camera. A phone photo works perfectly.

Step 2

Select Type & Extract

Tell the AI what kind of jewellery you're shooting — necklace, earrings, ring, bracelet, and more. The AI then automatically extracts the jewellery from its background, isolating it cleanly regardless of what's behind it. No manual editing, no Photoshop required.

Step 3

Configure & Shoot

Pick your model, choose between a photo shoot or video reel, and select the background style — studio white, lifestyle, editorial, or custom. Hit render and the AI does the rest, compositing your jewellery onto the model with photorealistic lighting and detail.

Step 4

Download & Post

Get catalog-ready model photos, pure-white Amazon-compliant e-commerce stills, and lip-synced video reels in Hindi & English. Everything is brand-stamped (Pro and above) and ready to post directly to your website, Amazon listing, or Instagram.
